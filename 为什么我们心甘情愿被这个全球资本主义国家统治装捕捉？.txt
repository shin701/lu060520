为什么我们心甘情愿被这个全球资本主义国家统治装捕捉？
=============================
发布时间：2010-05-02 11:32
这个背后其实也是我们对于幸福的热烈追求：我们想享受人作为人之存在的那一开放（the Openning），我们想要隔离我们身上的动物行为，而用了工具、物件、小发明（小电器之类）、机运概率（房价与股对于我们个人命运的影响）、目标选择和各种技术到我们的生活中；我们的生活中因此已被这种小聪明玩意儿塞满。
是的，是我们很愿意地主动将我们身上的动物行为隔离了出去，我们只在暴力片和恐怖片里一见这样的我们的身上的动物性。不是霍普金斯式的食人魔被关进笼子里，实际上是我们自己将自己关进这个叫做景观-媒体-全球资本主义国家统治装置这个玻璃笼子里去了，是我们被关押着了。
我们怎么来解放自己呢？将我们自己弄出去后我们怎么来恢复、治疗并重新将我们自己放生呢？阿甘本的《什么是装置？》想要回答这些问题。
to capture, orient, determine, intercept, model, control, or secure the gestures, behaviors, opinions. or discourses of living
beings，这就是这个装置正在对我们做的。我们失去了主体，只成为装置内的运行号码： In the nuntruth of the subject,its own truth is no longer at stake. He who lets himself be captured by the ”cellular telephone” apparatus-whatever the intensity of the desire that has driven him-cannot acquire a new subjectivity, but
only a number through which he can, eventually, be controlled. The spectator who spends his evenings in front o f the television set only gets, in exchange for his desubjectificarion, the frustrated mask of the couch porato, or his inclusion in the calculation of viewership ratings.
对付这个装置的办法是：亵渎它：The problem of the profanation of apparatuses-that is to say, the restitution to common use of what has been captured and separated in themis, for this reason, all the more urgent. But this problem cannot be properly raised as long as those who are concerned with it are unable to intervene in their own processes of subjectification, any more than in their own apparatuses, in order to then bring to light the Ungovernable, which is the beginning and, at the same time, the vanishing point of every politics.

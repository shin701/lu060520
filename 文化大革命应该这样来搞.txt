文化大革命应该这样来搞
=============================
发布时间：2010-04-06 00:18
 This is how a true ”cultural revolution” should be conducted:
not by directly targeting individuals, endeavoring to ”reeducate” them,
to ”change their reactionary attitudes,” but by depriving individuals of support
in the ”big Other,” in the institutional symbolic order（齐泽克《激进政治前景》）.

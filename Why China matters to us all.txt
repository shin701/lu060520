Why China matters to us all
=============================
发布时间：2008-07-15 13:11
Currently, if China were to deliver even half of the UK’s per capita GDP, most of the world’s accessible energy and resources would be used up by the 2030s.
The Chinese have as much right as any one else to a comfortable life in which they can fulfil their potential. The task for everyone in the coming decades is to help them achieve this in a sustainable way.

Unlike some city dwellers who count on their country retreats as a salve for the stress of demanding careers, the Formans work almost as much as they rest at their second home.

In 1979 Mr. Forman discovered the Connecticut countryside through a friend, Mikhail Baryshnikov. ”He invited me up to his manor, and I couldn't believe how much the rolling hills and leafy forests reminded me of where I grew up in Czechoslovakia.”

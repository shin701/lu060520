党国，转眼就成为一个官僚国家资产阶级的乐园。共产党内成为培养资产阶级的真正温床
=============================
发布时间：2010-06-08 09:28
 文革做的是一种真正的共产主义政治。它宣布列宁式的政党已经过时。它将马克思主义政治提高了一个档次。它要实现真正的无产阶级专政治，来对付这个自称的共产党，这个在政治起义时和解放战争后不得不独立的党国，转眼就成为一个官僚国家资产阶级的乐园。共产党内成为培养资产阶级的真正温床。这一些，我们在今天体会只有更深。我们被一种主导的历史图谱dominant histography of the Cultural Revolution蒙蔽，在目前是根本无法开始讨论这个问题的。
巴迪厄：《主体理论》：But what testifies by way of forcing to the necessary extension of the “partifiable”—
like the denumerable in Eudoxus—is the Cultural Revolution in
China, which, having stumbled on the party in the fire of a communist
uprising, puts on the agenda the fact that the Leninist party is over.
The domain of Leninism makes no real place, when it comes to the party,
for the problem of communism as such. Its business is the state, antagonistic
victory. The Cultural Revolution begins the forcing of this uninhabitable
place. It invites us to name the post-Leninist party as the “party of the new
type,” the party for communism, on the basis of which to recast the entire
field of Marxist practice.

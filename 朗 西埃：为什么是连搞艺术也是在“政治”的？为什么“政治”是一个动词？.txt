朗 西埃：为什么是连搞艺术也是在“政治”的？为什么“政治”是一个动词？
=============================
发布时间：2008-03-26 10:50
  Art is not political owing to the messages and feelings that it conveys on the state of social and political issues. Nor is it political owing to the way it represents social structures, conflicts or identities . It is political by virtue of the very distance that it takes with respect to those functions . It is political insofar as it frames not only works or monuments , but also a specific space-time sensorium , as this sensorium defines ways of being together or being apart , of being inside or outside , in front of or in the middle of , etc. It is political as its own practices shape forms of visibility that reframe the way in which practices , manners of being and modes of feeling and saying are interwoven in a commonsense , which means a ”sense of the common” embodied in a common sensorium （艺术是政治的，这并不取处于它所传递的关于社会和政治问题现状的信息和情感。这也不是由于它再现了各种社会结构、冲突或身份。它是政治的，首先正是因为它与这些功能保持了距离。它是政治的，不光是因为它框定了作品或碑牌，更是因为它是一种特别的时-空感性空间sensorium,这一感性空间定义了我们共同相处或互隔互割、处于内部抑或外部、投前还是处后或是中间等等的方式。它是政治的，是因为，它自身的实践塑造了可见性的形式，而正是这些形式重新框限了各种实践、存在样态和感情和表达模式在常识或共同情感（commonsense）中相互交织的方式，这一commonsense可理解为体现于[艺术所表现的]共同的sensorium中的一种“共同感”。）.

The Year's Notable Eating
=============================
发布时间：2010-01-01 11:38
 [我是一个图片]
The John Dory opened in December 2008, and closed in August. But April Bloomfield, the chef and an owner, and Ken Friedman, another owner, and are making noises about reopening the restaurant in another space. So there’s some chance we’ll see this marvelous dish — a murderously rich oyster pan roast topped with toast slathered in uni butter — again.

劳伦斯的斯宾诺莎式伦理观
=============================
发布时间：2008-02-17 23:18
 To be alive ,to be urgent and insistent—that was not-to-be. The highest of all was to melt out into the darkness and sway there, identified with the great Being…。[《儿子与情人》，企鹅，1985年，285页]
To be rid of our individuality, which is our will,which is our effort—to live effortless, a kind of curious sleep—that is very beautiful, I think;that is our after-life—our immortality。[286页]

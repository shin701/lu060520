Baudrillard：拥有一切后生的绝望
=============================
发布时间：2010-11-25 23:15
 Almost everything is given to us. And we are entitled to it all. We are like slaves, bondservants whose lives have been spared but who are still bound by an intractable debt. At some point, though, that fundamental rule always applies and any positive transfer will be met with a negative reaction.

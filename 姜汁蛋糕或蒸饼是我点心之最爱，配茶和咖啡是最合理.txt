姜汁蛋糕或蒸饼是我点心之最爱，配茶和咖啡是最合理
=============================
发布时间：2009-08-01 15:01
 [我是一个图片]
This ginger cake is yummy, and so easy. When people ask for the recipe I have to admit that it’s my sister Sheila’s. You can serve it on its own, or with caramelised bananas and home-made ginger ice cream on top. Susan Price, Torquay
Ingredients
225g (8oz) butter
225g (8oz) dark brown sugar
225g (8oz) treacle1 cups (12 oz) plain flour
2 tsp ground ginger
2 eggs
250ml ( pint) milk
2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
Stem ginger (optional)
Method
1. Line a 9in (22cm) cake tin.
2. Place the butter, sugar and treacle into a pan and warm it through.
3. Stir in the flour and ground ginger until smooth.
4. Add the two eggs, milk and bicarb and mix thoroughly. You can add a pinch of stem ginger.
5. Place the mixture into the cake tin and bake in the middle of the oven for 50 minutes at 150C/gas 2.
6. Once cooked, turn the oven off and leave the door closed until it cools down.

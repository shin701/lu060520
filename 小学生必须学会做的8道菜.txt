小学生必须学会做的8道菜
=============================
发布时间：2008-01-23 20:33
英国小学里强制的做饭课程，课程美其名叫food technologies：
A：1. Fresh tomato sauce for pasta（拌面） (olive oil, garlic, fresh tomatoes and basil罗勒) 
2. Roast chicken and veggies 
3. Omelette（将洋葱等炒蛋后焖） (with all its variations) 
4. Fresh vegetable soup 
5. Salads/dressings 
6. Fruit compote （将各种水果切好用色拉酱盖）
7. Stir fry （象中国人那样来油炒）
8. Beef stew（牛肉放水燉）
B：1. Chili con carne？？ with rice (peferably brown rice but white wd be a good starting point)/. For vegetarian option substitute orange lentils（细豆瓣） for the meat. 
2. Cauliflower with cheese sauce（在煮好的花菜上浇奶汁） 
3. Mashed potato, baked beans and grated cheese (my favourite school lunch) Delicious with a salad on the side. 
4. Lasagne（相当于外国的猪皮粉条） - meat , or vegetarian alternative. 
5. Jacket potato with variety of toppings: tuna, cheese, beans. Salad on the side （用微波炉将土豆烤熟中间夹鱼或肉）
6. Stir fried vegetables（炒素）: carrot, onion, brocolli (cabbage, peas) + teryaki/oyster sauce served with rice and grilled meat/fish/veg burger. 
7.Pasta bake（烤面条盖奶）, with vegetables mixed in. Homemade tomato sauce. topped with cheese. 
8.Roast chicken +potatoes+2 veg+gravy（肉汁）
C：1. Roast chicken, including veg and gravy (to learn timings) 
2. Pizza using a scone dough base (making tomato topping sauce from scratch = pasta sauce in another incarnation or spag bol) 
3. A curry咖哩, probably vegetable (learning different spices and that long lists of ingredients aren't that scary) 
4. Fish pie 
5. Beef stir fry 
6. A basic vegetable soup (if you can make one, you can make them all) 
7. Apple crumble（苹果切丁来烤脆） 
8. Victoria sponge（面粉加膨粉烤成饼）
D：
1. cous cous（摩洛哥式酿小米饭） with vegetables 
2. omelette and salad 
3. a soup of some kind 
4. pizza with choice of toppings 
5. baked chips with a dip 
6. shephards pie （烤饼里放肉鱼虾） 
7. pasta with tomato and hidden veg sauce 
8. stir fried rice with fish and green veg炒饭

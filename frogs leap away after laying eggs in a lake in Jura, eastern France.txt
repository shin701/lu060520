frogs leap away after laying eggs in a lake in Jura, eastern France
=============================
发布时间：2010-08-18 23:20

A group of mating frogs leap away after laying eggs in a lake in Jura, eastern France, causing the spawn to scatter. Tadpoles usually hatch three weeks after the eggs are laid. Photograph: Michel Loup/Biosphoto

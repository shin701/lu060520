Tester’s choice: Andrew Christian Dry AND Naked Brief $27, andrewchristian.com. The Andrew Christian brief sat low on his hips, “almost like low-rise jeans,” and at first that seemed like “a gimmick,” Mr. Krupa said. But on a run during a snowstorm, it turned out to be his favorite, with “a snug fit that kept everything in place” without being “restricting.” The fabric was “very warm” yet breathable, he said. Mr. Krupa wears boxers by day and said that these briefs “weren’t for the modest,” but were “very slimming.”

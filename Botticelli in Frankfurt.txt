Botticelli in Frankfurt
=============================
发布时间：2009-12-10 15:53
 [我是一个图片]
”In his 'Idealized Female Portrait' — what is it, piety? The reverse? That portrait is said to depict Simonetta Vespucci, who died of tuberculosis in 1476, plunging all Florentines, it was said, into mourning.”Supposedly, Botticelli pined for her ever after, bringing her back again and again in his pictures as the unattainable object of his dreams.”
